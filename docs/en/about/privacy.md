## What we collect

We collect personal data through a single subscription form hosted on Microsoft Forms. The form opens pre-selected for one of two purposes depending on which call-to-action you used:

- **Subscribe to the feed** (full data access request).
- **Register your interest** (newsletter / expression of interest only).

Fields collected: your name, e-mail, and, for data access requests, your organisation, role, and a description of intended use of the data.

We use **[GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/)**, a cookieless and privacy-respecting analytics tool, to count page views and see which pages and referrers are popular. No cookies are set, no personal data is collected, and IP addresses are not retained. See [GoatCounter's privacy statement](https://www.goatcounter.com/help/privacy) for details.

The subscription form is hosted on `forms.office.com`; submitting it is also governed by [Microsoft's privacy statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/).

## Why we collect it and on what basis

**Subscribe to the feed**

- **Purpose:** identify subscribers, provision API tokens, and provide operational communication (service alerts, format updates, planned downtime).
- **Legal basis:** processing necessary for steps taken at your request before entering into a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), and our legitimate interest in vetting access to a non-production service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

**Register your interest**

- **Purpose:** keep you informed about pilot status, format changes, and the post-pilot decision.
- **Legal basis:** your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given by submitting the form. You can withdraw it at any time.
