# Issue tracker & discussions

We use **GitHub** for public feedback on the pilot service and this site.

## Where to file what

- 🐞 **Bug reports, specific defects, broken links, incorrect documentation**
  → [Open an issue](https://github.com/tamtamresearch/tpeg.dopravniinfo.cz/issues)

- 💬 **Questions, ideas, use-case discussions, feedback on the data itself**
  → [Start a discussion](https://github.com/tamtamresearch/tpeg.dopravniinfo.cz/discussions)

- 🔒 **Anything sensitive** (credentials, contractual matters, personal data)
  → E-mail [tpeg@ceda.cz](mailto:tpeg@ceda.cz) directly.

## Before you file

A good report saves a round-trip. If you can, include:

- the **format and version** you are working with (e.g. `x-format:cz-ndic-tpeg2-tfp-v0.1`);
- a **minimal sample** of the data that triggers the issue;
- the **expected** vs. **observed** behaviour;
- your **client / library** and its version.

We monitor issues during Czech business hours; this is a pilot service and response times are not guaranteed.
